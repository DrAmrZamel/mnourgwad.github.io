simplify((x^3+2*x^2+5*x+10)/(x^2+5))

x^2*y^3 + 2*x^2 + 5*y^3 + 10

x + 2

Symbolic substitution

E=a*sin(b); subs(E,{a,b},{x,2})

E=x^2+6*x+7; subs(E,x,2)

 
ans =
 
x*sin(2)
 
 
ans =
 
23
